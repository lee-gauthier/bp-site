bio

Bridal Party's music is one part cluelessness, one part fear and one part absurd joy. It's all about living, creating and feeling. They rearrange their worst and best moments into pop music they like.

Bridal Party has released two EPs; toured across Canada; played with The Courtneys, Faith Healer, and Peach Pit; and performed at Sled Island 2018.

They are currently working on their debut full length album.
